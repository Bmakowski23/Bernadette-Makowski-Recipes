"How to Cook a Moose Bone"

Select the moose leg bone you want to cook. We are going to use the stove to cook our moose bone instead of the campfire.

Instructions

Defrost your moose bone, overnight in the fridge or in the sink.

Wash any debris off your moose bone.

Plain: wrap your bone with tin foil; place on the pan and throw in the oven at 375 F for 2.5 hours.

Flavour: Use your favourite spices on the moose bone; wrap your bone with tin foil and place onto the plan. Please it in the oven at 375 F for 2.5 hours.

Suggest spices: My aunt suggested that you can sprinkle a package of Lipton onion package on the bone. You can also use garlic salt or onion salt or pepper on the bone.

Boil some potatoes before it is cooked.

Make some fresh oven bannock before it is cooked.

Take it out the bone when done, and cut it up and enjoy!

Don't forget to turn off the oven.

Also, you could eat the bone marrow. Get an axe and chop the bone in half. Grab a long knife or utensil to scrap the bone marrow out of the bone.

Do not eat too much marrow; otherwise, you will be paying the consequences.

Elders Tell Us Children

When it came to yummy treats from the land like bone marrow, the elders would tell us children that it was not good for us. We only could have a little bit like a finger tip size. They want to save it for themselves because they are older, it is harder on their bodies compare to the children.
